South Park is an American animated series, created by Trey Parker and Matt Stone that is broadcast on Comedy Central in America and various other channels in other countries.

The series began in 1997 and is on its twentieth season, with a total of 277 episodes at seasons end, one movie, and 7 shorts, as of December 2, 2016. It has also won five Emmy Awards and has been nominated for various other awards.

The series revolves mainly around four schoolboys; Stan Marsh, Kyle Broflovski, Eric Cartman, and Kenny McCormick, but in later years it has introduced a number of other characters. The show has also caused a number of controversies regarding its content, as well as the parodies of people and events it portrays.

Along with The Simpsons and Arthur, South Park is the third longest-running cartoon in America.
